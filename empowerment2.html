GOV ENO DISBURSES ₦750M BUSINESS GRANTS TO 15,000 UNEMPLOYED YOUTHS IN AKWA IBOM

Governor Umo Eno Empowers Akwa Ibom Youths with Innovative Grant Program

As part of his administration’s commitment to youth empowerment and economic growth, Governor Umo Eno has launched an innovative grant program providing ₦50,000 monthly grants to 5,000 beneficiaries. Over the next 12 months, this initiative will expand to include 60,000 youths across Akwa Ibom’s 31 Local Government Areas, reflecting the administration's dedication to capacity building and economic inclusion.

Official Grant Presentation

During the official presentation of the grants at the Banquet Hall of Government House, Uyo, Governor Eno emphasized the program’s purpose: empowering youths to engage in productive ventures that enhance personal growth and contribute to the state’s economic development. He urged beneficiaries to use the funds wisely to maximize their impact and foster sustainable economic progress.

Broader Empowerment Initiatives

The grant program is part of a broader strategy under Governor Eno’s administration to empower Akwa Ibom youths. Other initiatives include:
• Ibom LED: Focused on leadership and entrepreneurial development.
• Dakkada Skills Acquisition Centre: Offering vocational training to equip youths with marketable skills.
• Ibom Community Watch: Engaging youths in security and intelligence roles to promote community safety.

Commitment to Measurable Results

Governor Eno views the grant disbursement as more than financial support. It signifies his administration’s commitment to delivering on promises through measurable results. By integrating programs like Ibom LED and the Dakkada Skills Acquisition Centre, the government is fostering a culture of innovation, self-reliance, and sustainable development among the youth.

This initiative underscores the governor’s vision of turning Akwa Ibom into a model of youth-driven economic growth and capacity building. Through strategic investments in the youth, Governor Umo Eno is laying a solid foundation for a prosperous and inclusive future for the state.
