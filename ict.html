NSIT UBIUM COUNCIL CHAIRMAN IMMORTALIZES LATE FIRST LADY: NAMES ICT COMPLEX IN HER HONOR

A state-of-the-art ICT facility, the Patience Umo Eno Information and Communication Technology Centre, has been inaugurated in Nsit Ubium Local Government Area. The center, located at the Council Secretariat, has the capacity to accommodate over 100 users and includes offices within the building.

Governor Umo Eno, represented by the Commissioner for Local Government and Chieftaincy Affairs, Mr. Frank Archibong, described the project as a significant milestone by the outgoing administration of Architect Iniobong Orok. He noted that naming the center after the late First Lady of the state, Patience Umo Eno, serves as a fitting tribute to her compassionate and caring legacy.

The Governor encouraged local residents to utilize the facility to enhance their knowledge in the ICT sector, highlighting the numerous life-changing opportunities available in the field. Architect Iniobong Orok, Chairman of the Transition Committee for Nsit Ubium, stated during the ceremony that the ICT center was built to inspire the youth to explore the vast potential of the technology sector. He expressed confidence that Nsit Ubium would soon become a notable hub for technological innovation.

The outgoing Chairman, joined by his wife Emem, expressed gratitude to Governor Umo Eno for the opportunity to serve in his administration. He also offered prayers for the Governor’s continued strength and comfort during this time of loss.
